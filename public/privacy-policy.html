Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 29, 2024

Introduction

KC's Diary ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal diary application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection and Storage

All your data stays on your device. KC's Diary stores all diary entries, photos, voice notes, and settings locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.

Information We Access

The app may request access to:

  • Camera: To take photos for your diary entries (optional)
  • Microphone: To record voice notes (optional)
  • Location: To add location tags to diary entries (optional)
  • Storage: To save and export your diary data
  • Notifications: To send daily reminders (optional)

All this data remains on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Open-Meteo API: For weather information. Only your approximate location coordinates are sent to fetch weather data. No personal information is shared.
  • Google Fonts: For typography. Standard web requests are made to load fonts.

Data Security

Your diary can be protected with a PIN lock and biometric authentication (fingerprint/face). The PIN is stored securely on your device.

Data Export and Backup

You can export your diary data as a backup file. This file is created locally and you control where it is saved or shared.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for personal use by individuals who can provide their own consent.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app's support channels.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your data. You can:

  • View all your data within the app
  • Export your data at any time
  • Delete your data by clearing app data or uninstalling the app
